The Venue

Moscone West, 800 Howard Street, San Francisco

Moscone Center is the largest convention and exhibition complex in San Francisco, California. Moscone West, where Infosys Confluence 2017 will take place, is a freestanding building and is almost as large as one city block. It comprises three levels that are 110 feet above street level with about 300,000 sq. ft. of space. Moscone West is in the heart of South of Market Area and is a short walk from hotels, restaurant and downtown Union Square.

Interesting Fact

The largest city-owned solar power system in the United States is located at the Moscone Center.
